Our Story

A Family Dream Come True

scroll down

Urbane Eatery is the product of our family’s love for food, friends,
and the beauty of the Montreal. We pride ourselves in combining fresh Urban cuisine,
a breathtaking view of the Mont Royal, and impeccable service
to give you a world-class culinary experience.

Our Chef

Meet Sebastian Michaelis

Dedicated to the culinary philosophy of “keep it fresh, keep it local”, Chef Sebastian Michaelis has created his own unique approach to modern Urban cuisines here at Montreal.

More About Our Chef

Chef Sebastian showcases his culinary expertise and creativity with every dish. Whether you are a seafood lover, steak lover, or a vegetarian, there is something for you at Urbane Eatery.

His creativity shines through in our daily specials that are crafted to use fresh, hand-picked ingredients depending on the season and availability.
